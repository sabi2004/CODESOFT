VARIETIES

Vennila Cake

Vennila cake, or vanilla cake, is a classic dessert known for its simple yet delightful flavor. Made with flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla extract, it has a light, fluffy texture and a sweet, aromatic taste.

Black Forest

Black Forest cake is a classic German dessert known for its rich layers of chocolate sponge cake, whipped cream, and cherries.

Red Velvet

Red velvet cake is a distinctive dessert known for its striking red color and smooth, velvety texture. Made with cocoa, buttermilk, and vinegar. Typically topped with cream cheese frosting, its unique taste and appearance make it a popular choice for celebrations.

Butter Cake

Butter cake is a rich, classic dessert known for its buttery flavor and moist texture. Made with simple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and a generous amount of butter, it's a versatile cake often enjoyed plain or with various toppings. Its comforting taste makes it a beloved treat for all occasions.

Strawberry Cake

Strawberry cake is a delightful dessert featuring fresh or pureed strawberries, which infuse it with a fruity flavor and pink hue.

Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a beloved dessert made from cocoa powder or melted chocolate, offering a rich, indulgent flavor. Its moist, tender crumb pairs perfectly with various frostings, especially chocolate ganache or buttercream.

HOW TO MAKE HOMEMADE CAKE?



TIPS FOR MAKING


To make a homemade cake, start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream 1 cup of sugar with 1/2 cup of butter until fluffy. Add 2 eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in 1.5 cups of flour and 1.75 teaspoons of baking powder. Finally, stir in 1/2 cup of milk until smooth. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake for 30-40 minutes. Allow the cake to cool before serving. Enjoy!
